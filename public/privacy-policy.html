Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Climb-or-Fall. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you play our game.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Climb-or-Fall does NOT collect, store, or process any personal identifiable information (PII) on our servers. All game progress, scores, and unlocked items are stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage.

3. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect personal data, the game utilizes third-party services that may collect information for advertising and analytics:

  • Google AdSense: Used to display banner advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to our site or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Unity Ads: Used to provide rewarded video ads for in-game revives. Unity may collect device identifiers and interaction data to serve relevant ads.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer at their official GitHub repository.
